FAQ

What's in a name?

zēˌrōTH (Zero + th) - immediately preceding what is otherwise regarded as first in a series.
Traditionally, getting a patent is regarded as the first step in profiting from innovation.
We help you take that first step with confidence by making sure that you are spending your time and money on innovation that will sell.

Why before I get a patent?

Not all novel inventions will sell. Not all innovations need a patent. Since the priority date is from the date of filing, we want you to have priority on the most valuable features from the earliest date possible.

What if I already have a patent?

We can help you determine if you should file more applications if you should cut your losses, or the best strategy for bringing the technology to market.

Confidentiality

We take confidentiality seriously. We will never disclose client names or how the technology works. To get you the maximum value from our service we just need to know what your innovation does, not how it does it.

Story Behind ZEROTH

I have watched too many inventors, companies, and investors put their time and money into innovations that will not sell.

Both large companies and single inventors burn through large sums of money filing for patent after patent. Ultimately, most of those expenses will never be recovered.

In my experience writing patents, reviewing startup investment pitches, and working in startups, I have seen that the traditional approach is flawed. However, there is a better, more efficient way to validate new technology and build a strong patent portfolio to protect it.

This is why I started ZEROTH. To help innovators and investors verify if new technology will sell and how they should focus their time and money developing it.
